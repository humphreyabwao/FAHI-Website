Step 3: Admissions Review & Guidance

Once approved, you receive confirmation and onboarding guidance including reporting instructions, timetable, and any practical or clinical preparation requirements for your course.

Admissions Experience

A supportive path from application to class entry

FAHI admissions is designed to be clear, responsive, and student-centered. From your first payment and form submission to final onboarding, our team guides you step by step.

  • Fast review of applications and supporting documents
  • Guidance on programme fit and learning mode selection
  • Clear onboarding support before classes begin

Guided Support: Admissions assistance by phone, email, and online form follow-up.

Practical Readiness: Health-track students receive clear skills and clinical preparation guidance.

Entry Requirements

  • Course-specific academic qualifications as required by each programme.
  • Good command of spoken and written English for learning, exams, and clinical communication.
  • Valid identification and required admission documents.
  • Access to a smartphone or computer for Hybrid and Online learning activities.
  • For health-related programmes, readiness for practical learning and clinical conduct standards (including safety and professionalism).

Fees & Scholarships

Tuition fees vary by programme and duration. For current fee structures, instalment options, and any available scholarship or support opportunities, contact FAHI admissions directly.
