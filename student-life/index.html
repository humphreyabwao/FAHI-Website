First American Health Institute

Student life & our campuses

A supportive community, real-world clinical experience, and three welcoming learning centres across Kenya — so you can focus on becoming the healthcare professional you want to be.

Overview

Whether you join us in Nairobi, Voi, or Mombasa, you will find structured skills labs, hybrid and on-campus sessions, and staff who know you by name.

Student life at FAHI blends academic rigour with mentorship, peer study groups, and preparation for local and international career pathways.

On campus & online

Your experience at FAHI

Learning that goes beyond the lecture — collaboration, clinical confidence, and career-ready habits.

Community & peer learning

Study alongside classmates who share your goals. FAHI encourages group revision, skills-practice pairs, and respectful dialogue across cultures — so you build communication skills that patients and employers notice.

Hands-on clinical confidence

Practical sessions and hospital attachments are designed to mirror real workflows — from vital signs and documentation to teamwork in busy units. You graduate with habits that transfer straight into the workplace.

Mentorship & career readiness

Faculty and coordinators guide you on placements, professional conduct, and next steps — including international pathways where relevant. Workshops on CVs, interviews, and exam preparation help you plan with clarity.

Hybrid learning that fits real life

Live online classes, recordings, and campus lab days work together — so you can balance family, work, and study while still meeting practical requirements for your programme.
