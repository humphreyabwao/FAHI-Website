Local & International Staffing Solutions

We provide recruitment support for Registered Nurses (RNs), Medical Laboratory Scientists (MLS), Medical Doctors (MDs), Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs), Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs)/Caregivers, Truck Drivers, and Graduate Teachers from Kenya and across Africa.

We support staffing in Kenya and international placements across the USA, Canada, Ireland, Finland, Germany, the Gulf countries, and the UK.

  • High-demand roles include RNs, CNAs/HCAs, and Medical Lab Professionals.
  • We collaborate with international recruiters for compliant and efficient staffing.
  • Kenyan hospitals can partner with FAHI to hire trained alums and senior students for placement.
