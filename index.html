Our Programs

What You Can Study at FAHI

At First American Health Institute (FAHI), we offer a diverse range of healthcare, technical, and professional development programs designed to equip students with practical skills and globally competitive qualifications.

Our courses include nursing and clinical training, emergency and critical care programs, specialized medical technician courses, community health and caregiving studies, international exam preparation, language training, ICT and technical skills development, as well as short professional certifications.

Each program is structured with a strong focus on hands-on experience, industry relevance, and career readiness to prepare students for both local and international healthcare opportunities.

International Pathways

Build a Healthcare Career Beyond Borders

At First American Health Institute (FAHI), our International Pathways program is designed to open doors for students who aspire to build healthcare careers beyond borders. We prepare learners for global opportunities through structured exam preparation, language training, and career development programs aligned with international healthcare standards.

Through partnerships, workforce development initiatives, and professional guidance, we support qualified graduates in accessing opportunities in countries such as the USA, Canada, the UK, Ireland, Germany, and the Gulf region.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between local training and global healthcare employment, empowering students to become competitive professionals in the international job market.
